# Prerequisites

- Completion of Session 2 (Foundry Models I) — you need a primary model choice (and ideally a fallback) with your own comparison notes
- A Foundry project with quota available for your chosen model in your target region
- Awareness that this session provisions a real, billable resource — plan to complete the cleanup step (`06-cleanup.md`) in the same sitting rather than leaving a deployment running

## Before you start

Have your Session 2 comparison table open. You'll deploy your primary model choice first, and this session assumes you already know why you picked it — if you skipped Session 2's comparison step, go back and do it before spending money on a deployment you haven't justified yet.
